Planning & Design

An Alvis event is more than an event – it is an interactive experience about who you are.

The Alvis team is a leader in the event industry for over 30 years internationally. Our specialty is creating one-of-a-kind celebrations that truly represent the honoree(s). It is through the details in every aspect of an event; from fine stationery, flowers, visual design, production, entertainment, lighting, to overall execution that an event can be the perfect medium to tell your celebration story. Alvis believes in the power of storytelling and aims to evoke emotion and memories for not only his clients but all of their guests as well.
